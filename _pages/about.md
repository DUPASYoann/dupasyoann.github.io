Passionate about innovation and new technologies, I am an engineer and researcher specializing in computer vision and deep learning. My expertise includes the development of advanced multimodal fusion models (RGB, infrared, LiDAR) as well as the optimization of embedded solutions for real-time applications.

On the scientific front, I actively explore agent-based AI approaches, Large Language Models (LLMs), and World Models—fields that are redefining the boundaries of artificial intelligence.

Outside of work, I pursue my passion for hiking, rock climbing, and video games. I have also developed a keen interest in FPV drones, a discipline that combines technology and adrenaline.
